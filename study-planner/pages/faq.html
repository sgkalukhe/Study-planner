Frequently Asked Questions

📝 How do I create a new study plan?

Go to the Dashboard and click "Create Plan". Choose your subjects and time slots to generate your plan.

🔐 Can I change my password?

Yes! Head over to the Settings page and update your password under "Account Info".

📊 How do I track my progress?

Use the Progress Tracking page to view your completed tasks and subject stats.

💡 How do I get personalized suggestions?

Enter your academic goals and subjects during setup. The system uses this to recommend effective schedules.

Feel free to contact us and we'll get back to you soon.
